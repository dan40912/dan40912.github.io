# Internal Industry Card Image Prompts

Use one consistent editorial-photography style across all images:

- Photorealistic professional Asian financial-service staff, warm but serious, no celebrity likeness.
- Premium internal SaaS / enterprise banking mood, soft daylight, light green brand accents, clean office or service environment.
- Horizontal website card image, 16:9 composition, subject placed slightly off-center with enough negative space.
- No logos, no readable UI text, no fake brand names, no watermarks, no distorted hands, no exaggerated smiles.
- Clothing: modern business casual or formal finance attire.
- Mood: trusted, capable, human, respectful.
