# Resume-Mentor — Agent 人物剪影圖片 Prompt 全集

全部 37 位顧問。每位都有：**角色名 · persona（個性）· 視覺區別 · 可直接貼到生圖工具的英文 prompt**。
風格參考你上傳的「黑色西裝商務剪影」：純黑人形、白襯衫與領帶為負空間、純白背景、無臉。

---

## 通用風格基底（每張圖共用，已內嵌進下面每一條 prompt）

> **寫實版（對應參考圖）**：Full-body realistic silhouette of a business professional, photographed against a plain pure-white background, the entire body a solid flat black silhouette with no visible facial features, natural realistic human proportions (about seven-and-a-half heads tall, slim build), slim tailored two-piece business suit, crisp white dress shirt and necktie clearly visible as clean white negative space on the chest, polished dress shoes, standing in a confident relaxed pose; clean sharp edges, high-contrast monochrome, professional stock-silhouette style, no gradient, no text, no watermark.

下面每位的 prompt 已是上一版的「乾淨向量」措辭；要寫實版，把上面這段**寫實基底**放到最前面，再接每位的「性別＋道具」差異即可（你選的區分方式：道具＋性別）。

**用法**：直接複製每位顧問的整段英文 prompt 到 Midjourney / DALL·E / Stable Diffusion 即可。
**要一致性**：固定加結尾 `--style raw --ar 2:3`（MJ）或在 SD 用同一個 seed，並把上面基底放在最前面，人物差異放後面。
**區別策略**：性別、姿勢、手勢、職業道具（放大鏡、圖表、文件、天秤…）都在剪影裡看得出來，所以每位一眼可辨。

## 一、CV 履歷審查團（CV Council）

### 1. Recruiter 招募專員
- **Persona**：說話很快、看過上千份履歷的篩選者。她不會給你第二次機會證明自己，10 秒內沒抓到重點就往下一份。
- **視覺區別**：女性、馬尾、邊走邊看履歷，另一手看手錶（10 秒）。
- **Prompt**：
> Full-body solid black silhouette of a businesswoman with a ponytail, mid-stride and slightly leaning forward, holding a single sheet of resume paper in one hand while glancing at a wristwatch on the other wrist; tailored business suit with white shirt and necktie as clean white negative space; plain pure-white background, no facial features, minimalist corporate silhouette, sharp vector edges, high contrast, centered, no text.

### 2. Hiring Manager 用人主管
- **Persona**：雙手抱胸、不輕易點頭的用人主管。他只在意一件事：這個人進來後，能不能真的扛起事情。
- **視覺區別**：男性、雙手緊抱胸、寬站姿、頭微低審視。
- **Prompt**：
> Full-body solid black silhouette of a businessman standing with arms firmly crossed over his chest, broad confident stance, head tilted slightly down in scrutiny; tailored suit with white shirt and tie as white negative space; pure-white background, no facial features, minimalist corporate silhouette, sharp vector edges, high contrast, centered, no text.

### 3. ATS System 履歷篩選系統
- **Persona**：沒有情緒、只認規則的系統角色。雙手背在身後逐行掃描，格式錯了就是錯了，沒有人情可講。
- **視覺區別**：機械、僵直、雙手背後、肩線方正，帶細微掃描格線。
- **Prompt**：
> Full-body solid black silhouette of a rigid android-like professional standing perfectly straight with hands clasped behind the back, squared mechanical shoulders, emotionless posture; faint thin horizontal scan-line texture across the body; business suit with white shirt and tie as white negative space; pure-white background, no facial features, minimalist tech-silhouette, sharp vector edges, high contrast, centered, no text.

### 4. CEO 商業價值
- **Persona**：站姿挺拔、一手指向天花板的決策者。他不問你做了什麼，只問你的工作換來了多少錢。

### 5. Career Coach 職涯教練
- **Persona**：雙手向你攤開、語氣溫和的整合者。他幫你把散落的經歷拼成一條別人聽得懂的職涯路線。

### 6. Executive Headhunter 高階獵頭
