Word Scramble Instructions

Want to Make Your Own Word Scramble?

Step 1: Modify the Google Spreadsheet Template

  • Make a copy of this template. (You'll need to sign-in with your Google account.)
  • Edit the words.
  • To add a final phrase challenge at the bottom of your word scramble, type a hashtag (#) at the beginning of any entry.
  • Label your word scramble by changing the name of the worksheet (at the bottom).
  • Do not edit any cell with a blue background.

Step 2: Publish Your Spreadsheet

  • Go to File, Publish to the Web…, then click Publish.

Step 3: Get Your Flippity.net Link

  • Click on the Get the Link Here tab of the template (at the bottom).
  • Click on the Flippity.net link to see and print out your word scramble.
    • Press the reload button to re-scramble the words.
    • Press the key button to see and print the answer key.

Step 4: Bookmark and Share

  • Bookmark the page to find it again quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Word Scramble be played online?

  • Word scrambles are designed to be printed, not completed online.

Just how scrambled will each word get?

  • Scrambling is truly random which means there's a slim chance that a word could end up unchanged. If this happens just press the reload button to re-scramble.
  • Flippity has been programmed to ensure scrambled words don't incidentally create profanity.
