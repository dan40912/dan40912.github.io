Certificate Quiz Instructions

Want to Make Your Own Certificate Quiz?

Step 1: Modify the Google Spreadsheet Template

  • Make a copy of this template. (You'll need to sign-in with your Google account.)
  • Edit the text in the Quiz Questions tab.
    • In the Type column, enter 'Q' for each question and 'A' for each answer choice.
    • Enter as many answer choices as you want.
    • Include an asterisk ('A*') to indicate each correct response.
    • You may only specify one correct answer for each question.
  • Click the Options tab at the bottom of the template to change the quiz settings.
    • quiz name: enter a name for your quiz
    • shuffle questions: enter 'yes' or 'no'
    • shuffle choices: enter 'yes' or 'no'
    • email results: enter 'yes' or 'no'
    • email name: enter the name you wish to appear on email confirmation message
    • email address: address at which you wish to receive notifications
      • check spam, be sure you can receive messages from 'noreply@flippity.net'
    • certificate image: enter image URL for a custom image
      • must be 530 pixels wide by 100 pixels high
  • Do not edit any cell with a blue background. Do not change the order of the worksheets (tabs).

Step 2: Publish Your Spreadsheet

  • Go to File, Publish to the Web…, then click Publish.

Step 3: Get Your Flippity.net Link

  • Click on the Get the Link Here tab of the template (at the bottom).
  • Click on the Flippity.net link to test out your quiz.

Step 4: Bookmark and Share

  • Share the Flippity.net link with anyone you want to earn your certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I write my certificate quiz?

  • In the Type column type a Q to specify a question. In the adjacent Text column type your question.
  • Below each question type an A in the Type column to specify a multiple-choice answer option. In the adjacent Text column type your answer choice.
    • You may have as many answer options as you want for each question.
    • You need a minimum of one answer option for each question.
  • Type an asterisk (*) next to the A in the Type column to specify the correct answer.
    • Each question must have one, and only one, correct answer.
  • Repeat the process for each additional question.

How do I use pictures in my certificate quiz?

  • In the appropriate cell, type [[Image: followed by the URL (address) of a picture you find on the web followed by ]].
    • e.g. [[Image:https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/f/f0/Cheers_intro_logo.jpg]]
    • URLs must end in ".gif", ".png", ".jpg", ".jpeg", or ".webp".
    • You cannot use Google Drive to store your images.
    • You can use Google Drawings. Use the "Publish to the web…" link.
    • You may include text before or after the image.
    • Images are automatically resized to 200 pixels high for questions and 100 pixels high for answer options.
    • Smaller images load quicker.
    • (Be sure you have the legal right to use each image

How do I use YouTube videos in my certificate quiz?

  • In the appropriate cell, type [[ followed by YouTube's "Share this video" link followed by ]].
    • e.g. [[https://youtu.be/kwFvJog2dMw]]
    • You may include text before or after the video.

How many questions can I have in my certificate quiz?

  • Theoretically there is no limit, but the more questions you have the slower it will load and the more likely you are to run into trouble.
  • (You should be able to give a 50 question quiz without problems.)

How do I change the certificate quiz settings?

  • Click on the Options tab at the bottom of the spreadsheet.
  • Change the quiz name to whatever you'd like your certificate to be called.
  • Enter yes in shuffle questions if you'd like the questions to be in random order. Type no if you want them to be in the same order you wrote them.
  • Enter yes in shuffle choices if you'd like the multiple-choice options for each question to be in random order. Type no if you want them to be in the same order you wrote them.
    • Don't use shuffle if any of your options say "all of the above."
  • If you want users to be able to submit their results to you via email, enter yes next to email results.
    • Enter the name you want users to see next to email name.
    • Enter your email address next to email address you want the results sent to.
      • Check your spam filter.
      • Be sure you can receive messages from noreply@flippity.net.
    • The email results is not guranteed to work reliably. Do not use it for something important like a high-stakes assessment.

How do I make changes to my certificate quiz?

  • Make sure Automatically republish when changes are made is checked, then go ahead and make changes to your Google Spreadsheet.
  • Your changes will automatically be reflected, no need to get a new link.
